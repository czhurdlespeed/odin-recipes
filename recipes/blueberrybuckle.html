Blueberry Buckle

Description

Blueberry Buckle is a blueberry lemon cake topped with a brown sugar crumble

Ingredients

  • Flour, Sugar, Eggs, etc.
  • Blueberries
  • Lemon
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine Flour, Sugar, and Eggs
  2. Mix in Blueberries
  3. Make Brown Sugar Crumble
  4. Enjoy!
